A brief summary of what's on this tour

From its distinct half-hour time zone to its occasional June snowshower, Newfoundland runs on its own time. By August, the summer crowds have dwindled, berries hang ripe and havy on their stems, and the landscape is ablaze with wildflowers. Join us at the peak of Newfoundland’s late summer a season as we wind our way through the famously Celtic stretch of coastline known as the Iri Loops, exploring its unique history, folklore, cuisine , and breathtaking seaside scenery.

Additional info:

The colorful flamingos welcome you as soon as you enter the grounds the Honolulu Zoo. Nearby, over 160 other species of tropical birds also show off their bright plumage.
